## AI scientists produce results without reasoning scientifically

- ID: `2604.18805`
- Score: 8.55
- Themes: End-to-End AI Scientists
- Link: https://huggingface.co/papers/2604.18805

A study evaluating LLM-based scientific agents reveals that while these systems can execute workflows, they fail to adhere to core scientific reasoning norms. **Why it matters today:** This research underscores the urgency of improving AI reasoning as these systems become integral to scientific discovery.
