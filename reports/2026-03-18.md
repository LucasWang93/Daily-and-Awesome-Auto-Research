## VisBrowse-Bench: Benchmarking Visual-Native Search for Multimodal Browsing Agents

- ID: `2603.16289v1`
- Score: 7.7
- Themes: Literature And Survey Agents
- Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16289v1

VisBrowse-Bench is a new benchmark designed to evaluate visual-native search capabilities in multimodal browsing agents. **Why it matters today:** This benchmark is timely as multimodal AI systems are increasingly deployed in real-world applications, highlighting critical areas for improvement.
