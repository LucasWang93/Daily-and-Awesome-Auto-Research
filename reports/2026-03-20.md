## Total Recall QA: A Verifiable Evaluation Suite for Deep Research Agents

- Score: 8.55
- Themes: End-to-End AI Scientists, Literature And Survey Agents, Research Infrastructure And Benchmarks
- Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18516v1

Total Recall QA (TRQA) introduces a verifiable evaluation framework for deep research agents, focusing on multi-step reasoning and synthesis over large, open-domain sources. Built using Wikidata-Wikipedia and synthetic datasets, TRQA establishes baseline metrics for future comparisons. This benchmark aims to address gaps in current evaluation methods, ensuring more reliable assessments of AI research systems.

**Why it matters today:** With the rise of AI-driven research tools, robust evaluation frameworks like TRQA are essential to ensure trust and reliability in their outputs.
