TRACE is a cutting-edge multi-agent system that combines AI-driven planning with seismological constraints to autonomously interpret earthquake mechanisms. - Score: 7.8
- Themes: Autonomous Discovery
- Link: https://huggingface.co/papers/2603.19987

The findings highlight the importance of structured Markovian representations for fostering novel reasoning in generative AI.

**Why it matters today:** This work is timely as the demand for more autonomous and innovative AI systems continues to grow.
